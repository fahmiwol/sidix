### 2. End-to-End Multi-Modal Creation (note 248 hero use-case)

User brief: *"Maskot brand makanan ringan kawaii ulat kuning"*

- Landing page (copy + layout)

Persona-distinct (UTZ creative voice fundamental berbeda), forward-looking, intuitive — **bukan generic LLM output**.

### 3. Self-Evolving (4 Pilar BEBAS DAN TUMBUH — note 228)

- **Decentralized Dynamic Memory** — corpus + pattern library + skill library
- **Multi-Agent Adversarial** — Observer/Innovator/Critic loop

- **Proactive Triggering** — cron jobs, synthetic Q, autonomous research

Setiap kuartal SIDIX **lebih pintar dari kuartal sebelumnya**. Bukan snapshot, tapi makhluk hidup yang belajar.

### 4. 5 Persona Fundamental (DoRA, BUKAN Akting Prompt-Level)
