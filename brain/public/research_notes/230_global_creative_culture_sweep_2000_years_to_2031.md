# BAGIAN 1 — DESIGN STYLE EVOLUSI (2000 BC → 2026)

## 1.1 Ancient Era (2000 BC — 500 AD)

| Civilization | Karakter Visual | Legacy yang Masih Relevan |
|---|---|---|
| **Egypt** (3000 BC—) | Hieroglyphic, profile pose, geometric strict, gold + blue + red | Symmetry, monumental scale, brand consistency 3000 tahun |
| **Greek** (800 BC—) | Mathematical proportion (golden ratio), realistic anatomy, marble white | Vitruvian principles, classical typography |
| **Roman** (500 BC—476 AD) | Engineered functional, arch + dome, fresco mural | Civic design, Trajan font (still used 2026!) |
| **Maya** (2000 BC—) | Glyphic writing, jaguar/serpent motif, pyramid step | Glyph sebagai modular system, color science |
| **China Han** (200 BC—) | Brush calligraphy, scroll format, ink wash | Negative space mastery, vertical composition |
| **India Maurya** (300 BC—) | Buddhist iconography, lotus motif, bas-relief | Stupa form, mandala geometry |
| **Persia** (550 BC—) | Carpet pattern, mosaic, intricate symmetry | Arabesque, infinite tessellation |

**Lesson untuk SIDIX**: setiap civilization punya **visual grammar** distinct.
Modern designer (saat brief Egyptian-themed) sering trivialize jadi pyramid +
hieroglyph. Real grammar = symmetry rule + color palette specific +
proportion canon. SIDIX LoRA per civilization = fidelity tinggi.

## 1.2 Medieval (500 — 1400 AD)

| Style | Period | Karakter |
|---|---|---|
| **Byzantine** | 500-1450 | Gold leaf icon, flat perspective, religious narrative |
| **Romanesque** | 800-1200 | Heavy stone, rounded arch, illuminated manuscript |
| **Gothic** | 1140-1500 | Pointed arch, flying buttress, stained glass, vertical thrust |
| **Islamic Golden Age** | 750-1258 | Geometric tessellation, calligraphy, no figurative |
| **Chinese Tang/Song** | 618-1279 | Landscape painting peak, monochrome ink, scholar aesthetic |
| **Japanese Heian** | 794-1185 | Yamato-e, scroll narrative, refined court aesthetic |
| **Mesoamerican Postclassic** | 900-1521 | Aztec codex, Mexica geometric, color saturation |

**Lesson**: medieval era = **decorative density** mature. Modern design
revival 1900s (Arts & Crafts movement) bring back. Today: tattoo,
illuminated lettering, high-fantasy gaming all reference medieval.

## 1.3 Renaissance → Baroque (1400 — 1800)

- **Italian Renaissance** (1400-1600): perspective scientific, anatomy
  accurate, chiaroscuro, Vitruvian Man. Da Vinci, Michelangelo, Raphael.
- **Northern Renaissance** (1450-1600): oil painting mastery, detail
  obsessive, Bruegel peasant scenes, Dürer printmaking.
- **Baroque** (1600-1750): drama, motion, contrast, emotion.
  Caravaggio chiaroscuro, Rubens flesh, Bernini sculpture.
- **Rococo** (1730-1770): pastel, ornate, aristocratic playfulness.
- **Mughal/Persian/Ottoman** (1500-1800): miniature painting, court portraiture.
- **Edo Japan** (1603-1868): ukiyo-e, Hokusai/Hiroshige, woodblock print
  → influenced European Impressionism.

**Lesson**: Renaissance = **technical mastery** baseline yang AI sekarang
attempt re-create via Stable Diffusion. Ukiyo-e style = popular LoRA 2024+.

## 1.4 Modern (1800 — 1970)

| Movement | Year | Karakter | Influence Today |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Romanticism** | 1800-1850 | Emotion, nature sublime, individualism | Gothic novel revival |
| **Impressionism** | 1860-1890 | Light + color, brushwork visible (Monet, Renoir) | Photography-inspired digital art |
| **Art Nouveau** | 1890-1910 | Flowing organic line, plant motif (Mucha, Klimt) | Tattoo + branding revival |
| **Cubism** | 1907-1920 | Multi-perspective fragmented (Picasso) | Abstract digital art |
| **Bauhaus** | 1919-1933 | Function-form, geometric primary color | Modern UI/UX **foundation** ⭐ |
| **Art Deco** | 1920-1940 | Symmetric luxury geometric (Empire State, Chrysler) | Premium brand aesthetic |
| **Surrealism** | 1924-1950 | Dream logic (Dalí, Magritte) | AI art aesthetic dominant |
| **Abstract Expressionism** | 1940-1960 | Pollock drip, Rothko field | Contemporary fine art |
| **Mid-Century Modern** | 1945-1970 | Eames, Saarinen, organic clean | Apple/Google brand inspiration |
| **Pop Art** | 1955-1970 | Warhol, Lichtenstein, mass culture irony | Streetwear, meme culture root |

**Bauhaus (1919-1933) = TURNING POINT** untuk modern design. "Form follows
function" → minimalism dominate 100 tahun ke depan. Apple, Braun, IKEA all
descend dari Bauhaus.
